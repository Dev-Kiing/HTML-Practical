My Journey from Fashion to Tech

My name is King-Solomon O. Atebefia. I was once a fashion designer with no plans of touching tech. But in 2024, I took the leap, and what a journey it's been. My wife enrolled me at Welup Digital Academy to study UI/UX Design. At first, I failed. I dropped out of the 3-month bootcamp within two weeks due to my laziness and self-doubt.

But in 2025, I believed in myself enough to enroll again. This time around, I came back stronger. I amazed even my tutor. He often asked if he was really the one who taught me, because of how quickly I applied what I learned.

Guess what, I didn't stop there. I enrolled in another three-month tech skill (Frontend Web Development). I wanted to design and build. In just a few weeks, here I am mastering Git, GitHub, the command line, and VS Code, which I've learned so far. I've created real repositories, and today, I'm writing my first web page to be launched on the internet, this very one.

This transition wasn't just professional. It was personal. It required me to grow emotionally, mentally, and spiritually. Late nights, early mornings, self-discipline, and above all, the desire to make my wife proud and prove to myself I could do more.
